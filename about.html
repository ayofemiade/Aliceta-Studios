Meet Taiwo O. Oliyide

Driven by a love for visual storytelling, I transitioned from makeup artistry into photography, bringing a unique perspective that combines both art forms. My journey has been defined by a passion for capturing authentic moments and creating images that resonate with emotion and beauty.

As an entrepreneur and makeup instructor, I've had the privilege of training over a thousand makeup artists and teaching with prestigious programs like Daystar Skill Acquisition and House of Tara International in Lagos. This teaching experience has deepened my understanding of beauty, composition, and the transformative power of visual art.

I bring creativity, technical skill, and genuine care to every session. Whether it's a portrait that captures your essence, a wedding that tells your love story, or a fashion shoot that showcases bold creativity, I approach each project with meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to excellence.

My Approach

The art and science behind every shot

Storytelling

Every image should tell a story. I focus on capturing genuine emotions and authentic moments that reveal the true essence of my subjects.

Composition

Thoughtful composition creates visual harmony. I carefully consider light, space, and balance to create images that are both beautiful and impactful.

Color Theory

My background in makeup artistry gives me a unique understanding of color. I use this knowledge to create images with rich, harmonious palettes.

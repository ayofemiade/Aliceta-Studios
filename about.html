Our Story

How Aliceta Studios came to be

From Passion to Profession

Aliceta Studios was founded in 2015 by Alice Taylor, a passionate photographer with over 15 years of experience in portrait and fashion photography. What started as a small home studio has grown into a full-service photography business serving clients across the region.

Our journey began with a simple belief: that every person deserves to see themselves captured beautifully through professional photography. This philosophy continues to guide our work today as we help clients celebrate life's special moments and express their unique personalities through our lens.

Over the years, we've expanded our services to include wedding photography, fashion shoots, and makeup artistry, always maintaining our commitment to quality, creativity, and personalized service.
